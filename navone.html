Female Health Issues

Maternal deaths are similarly high. The reasons for this high mortality are that few women have access to skilled birth attendants and fewer still to quality emergency obstetric care. In addition, only 15 per cent of mothers receive complete antenatal care and only 58 per cent receive iron or folate tablets or syrup. Women's health in India involves numerous issues.
Some of them include the following:
1. Malnutrition : The main cause of female malnutrition in India is the tradition requiring women to eat last, even during pregnancy and when they are lactating.
2. Breast Cancer : One of the most severe and increasing problems among women in India, resulting in higher mortality rates.
3. Maternal Mortality : Indian maternal mortality rates in rural areas are one of the highest in the world.
